Connect two Beocreate 4-Channel Amplifiers together. Why and how.

Pairing Mode

How it works

Three-way or four-way speakers use more than two amplifier channels. Playing in stereo is not possible with such speakers using only one amplifier.

The pairing feature allows you to connect two amplifiers with an optical audio cable so that they work together, each powering one speaker.

When paired, one of the speakers is the master speaker that receives sound from your sources. The master speaker will then send sound to the slave speaker.
